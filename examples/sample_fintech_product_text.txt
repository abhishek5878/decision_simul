SavingsGoal - A goal-based savings app for young professionals in metro cities.

Product Description:
SavingsGoal helps users save money for specific goals (vacation, emergency fund, down payment) by linking their bank account via UPI, setting up automatic transfers, and tracking progress.

Target Users:
- Young professionals (25-35 years old) in Tier-1 metros
- Comfortable with digital apps and UPI
- Moderate to high income (middle to upper-middle class)
- Individualistic decision-makers (not heavily family-influenced)
- Growth-oriented and aspirational
- Price-conscious but not deal-only
- Balanced to risk-tolerant attitude (comfortable with digital finance)

Product Flow:
1. Landing page - Explains the concept of goal-based savings, shows example goals, emphasizes security and ease
2. Phone verification - User enters mobile number, receives OTP, verifies
3. KYC - User uploads PAN card and Aadhaar, takes selfie for verification
4. Bank account linking - User links primary bank account via UPI mandate (read-only access)
5. Goal creation - User creates first savings goal (amount, timeline, auto-transfer settings)
6. First transfer - User makes first manual transfer or sets up auto-transfer

Key Features:
- RBI-regulated, bank-grade security
- No fees, no minimum balance
- Instant goal tracking and progress visualization
- Auto-transfer options for hands-off savings
