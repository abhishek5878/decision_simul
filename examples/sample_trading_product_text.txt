TradeEasy - Zero-commission stock trading app for young investors

Product Description:
TradeEasy is a mobile-first stock trading platform offering zero-commission equity trading, mutual funds, and SIPs. Designed for first-time investors and young professionals who want to start investing with small amounts.

Target Users:
- Young professionals and students (20-35 years old) in metro cities
- Moderate to high income (middle to upper-middle class)
- Tech-savvy, comfortable with mobile apps
- Growth-oriented, interested in wealth building
- Moderate to high risk tolerance (comfortable with equity markets)
- Individual decision-makers
- High intent to start investing

Product Flow:
1. Landing page - Explains zero-commission trading, investment options, educational content
2. Phone + OTP - Mobile number verification
3. KYC - PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, selfie verification
4. Investment selection - Choose stocks, mutual funds, or SIPs (browse, search, compare)
5. Bank linking - Link bank account for funding (read-only access, UPI mandate)
