Primary target group:

- Age: 25-45 years old (young to middle-aged)
- Location: Tier-1 metros (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore) and Tier-2 cities
- Income: Middle to upper-middle class (₹25k-₹1.5L monthly)
- Digital comfort: Medium to high (comfortable with apps, UPI, netbanking)
- Risk attitude: Moderate to high (comfortable with unsecured loans)
- Intent: High (urgent need for credit, actively looking for loans)

- First-time borrowers who are new to credit
- Existing customers looking for top-up loans
