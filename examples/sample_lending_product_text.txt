QuickCredit - Instant personal loans for salaried professionals

Product Description:
QuickCredit offers instant personal loans up to ₹5 lakhs for salaried professionals. No collateral required, minimal documentation, and funds disbursed within 24 hours. Credit decisions based on salary slips and bank statements.

Target Users:
- Salaried professionals (25-45 years old) in Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities
- Monthly income ₹25k-₹2L
- Comfortable with digital apps and online banking
- Need quick access to credit for emergencies or planned expenses
- Moderate to high risk tolerance (comfortable with unsecured loans)
- Individual decision-makers (not heavily family-influenced)
- Growth-oriented, aspirational lifestyle

Product Flow:
1. Landing page - Explains loan features, eligibility, interest rates, EMI calculator
2. Phone verification - Enter mobile number, receive OTP, verify
3. KYC - Upload PAN card, Aadhaar, selfie for verification
4. Income verification - Upload salary slips (last 3 months) and bank statements
5. Credit check - System checks credit score, assigns loan limit
6. Loan agreement - Review terms, interest rate, repayment schedule, sign digitally
7. Bank account linking - Link bank account for disbursement (read-only access)
8. Disbursement - Funds transferred to linked account within 24 hours

Key Features:
- RBI-regulated NBFC
- No hidden charges, transparent interest rates
- Flexible repayment (12-60 months)
- Pre-approved limits for existing customers
- Instant credit decision
