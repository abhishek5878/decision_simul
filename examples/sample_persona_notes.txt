Our target users are:

1. Young salaried professionals (25-35 years old) in Tier-1 metros like Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore
   - Monthly income between ₹30k-₹1.5L
   - Comfortable with digital apps and online banking
   - Need quick access to credit for emergencies or planned expenses
   - Moderate to high risk tolerance (comfortable with unsecured loans)
   - Individual decision-makers (not heavily family-influenced)
   - Growth-oriented, aspirational lifestyle

2. Middle-aged salaried workers (35-45 years old) in Tier-2 cities
   - Monthly income between ₹25k-₹80k
   - Moderate digital comfort (use UPI, netbanking)
   - More cautious about loans but need credit for family expenses
   - Lower risk tolerance than young professionals
   - May consult family before taking loan
   - Stability-focused but still aspirational
