# Credigo Personas Guide

## Overview

This document defines 8 optimized personas specifically designed for Credigo.club (credit card recommendation product). Each persona represents a distinct user archetype with different motivations, behaviors, and intent profiles.

---

## 1. Young Professional Credit Seeker

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-30 years
- Location: Metro city
- Income: Upper-middle class (SEC: 0.7)
- Status: Single/independent

**Key Traits:**
- **Digital Literacy:** 0.85 (High - comfortable with apps)
- **Aspiration:** 0.9 (Very high - wants premium lifestyle)
- **Price Sensitivity:** 0.4 (Moderate - values quality over price)
- **Family Influence:** 0.4 (Moderate - independent decision maker)

**Primary Intent:** Compare Options (35%)
- Wants to compare multiple credit cards
- Learning about credit cards (25%)
- Quick decision maker (20%)

**Why They Use Credigo:**
- First-time credit card seeker
- Wants to make informed decision
- Values recommendations and reviews
- Tech-savvy, comfortable with digital tools

**Expected Behavior:**
- Will explore multiple options
- Needs clear comparison
- Moderate tolerance for effort
- Values quality and benefits over price
