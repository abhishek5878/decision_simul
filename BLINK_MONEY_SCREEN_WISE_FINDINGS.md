# Blink Money: Screen-Wise Findings & Improvement Recommendations

**Analysis Date:** January 2025
**Sample Size:** 100 personas Ã— 7 variants = 700 total trajectories
**Product:** Blink Money (7-step credit against mutual funds onboarding)

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis identifies screen-by-screen drop points, dominant failure forces, and actionable improvements for each step of the Blink Money onboarding flow.

**Overall Metrics:**
- Entry Rate: 52.5%
- Completion Rate: 40.4%
- Total Conversion: 21.2%

---

## Step 1: Smart Credit against Mutual Funds

### Current Performance

- **Users Reached:** 700
- **Users Dropped:** 143 (20.4%)
- **Users Continued:** 557 (79.6%)
- **Unique Personas Affected:** 143

### Why Users Drop at This Step

- **Value:** 42.9% of drop pressure
- **Trust:** 33.0% of drop pressure
- **Risk:** 12.8% of drop pressure
- **Cognitive Energy:** 9.2% of drop pressure
- **Risk Tolerance:** 2.0% of drop pressure

### What's Missing / What We Missed

**Issues Identified:**
- **Value Clarity Gap:** 20.4% drop rate indicates value proposition not immediately clear
- Landing page doesn't quickly communicate 'credit against mutual funds' benefit
- Missing explicit trust signals (security badges, partner logos)
- **Trust/Value Signal Weak:** 42.9% of drops due to value
- Missing urgency/scarcity signals that drive action
- No clear 'what happens next' expectation setting

### State Variant Performance

How different user states perform at this step:

| Variant | Reached | Dropped | Drop Rate | Continued | Continue Rate |
|---------|---------|---------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Browsing Casually | 100 | 26 | 26.0% | 74 | 74.0% |
| Price Sensitive | 100 | 25 | 25.0% | 75 | 75.0% |
| Tired Commuter | 100 | 22 | 22.0% | 78 | 78.0% |
| Distrustful Arrival | 100 | 22 | 22.0% | 78 | 78.0% |
| Fresh Motivated | 100 | 18 | 18.0% | 82 | 82.0% |
| Urgent Need | 100 | 17 | 17.0% | 83 | 83.0% |
| Tech Savvy Optimistic | 100 | 13 | 13.0% | 87 | 87.0% |

**Key Insights:**

- **Browsing Casually** has the highest drop rate (26.0%) at this step
- **Tech Savvy Optimistic** has the lowest drop rate (13.0%) at this step
- **High variant differentiation** (13.0 percentage points) - this step affects user states very differently
- **Price Sensitive** and **Browsing Casually** variants struggle most at entry (low value perception)
- **Tech Savvy Optimistic** and **Urgent Need** perform best (higher trust/value perception)

## Overall Recommendations Summary

### Priority 1: Trust & Value Communication
- Add trust badges and security messaging throughout
- Make value proposition crystal clear on landing page
- Explain 'why' for each data ask

### Priority 2: Reduce Friction
- Add progress indicators (Step X of 7)
- Provide 'why we need this' explanations
- Consider skip options for optional steps

### Priority 3: Context & Expectations
- Show 'what happens next' at each step
- Remind users of benefits (2-hour approval, low rates)
- Set expectations about process length

---

**Next Steps:**
1. Implement Priority 1 improvements (trust & value)
2. A/B test high-drop steps (Steps 1, 2, 3) 
3. Measure impact on completion rates
4. Iterate based on user feedback
